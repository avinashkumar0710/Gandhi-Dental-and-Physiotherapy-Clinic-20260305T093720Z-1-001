Welcome to Gandhi Dental & Physiotherapy Clinic

Located in the heart of Bhilai, Chhattisgarh, Gandhi Dental & Physiotherapy Clinic is a trusted name in comprehensive healthcare. We bring together two essential specialties — dental care and physiotherapy — under one roof to provide holistic health solutions for our community.

Our clinic is located at 20A/8, 20/A, Gurudwara Road, near Rawlani Medicals, Nehru Nagar West, making it easily accessible for patients across Bhilai and surrounding areas. Meet Our Doctor

Expert care you can trust

Dr. Nisha Gandhi

Physiotherapist | Clinic Lead

Dr. Nisha Gandhi is a highly experienced and dedicated physiotherapist leading the Gandhi Dental & Physiotherapy Clinic. With a deep commitment to patient care and wellness, she has built a reputation for delivering effective treatments with a compassionate and personal touch.

Her expertise spans a wide range of physiotherapy treatments including cryotherapy, heat therapy, TENS (Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation), and specialized care for spinal disorders, back pain, and joint pain. She is known for her thorough approach, taking time to understand each patient's condition and crafting personalized treatment plans.

Under her leadership, the clinic has achieved an impressive 4.9-star rating on Google from over 134 patient reviews — a testament to the quality of care and patient satisfaction that defines the clinic's approach.
