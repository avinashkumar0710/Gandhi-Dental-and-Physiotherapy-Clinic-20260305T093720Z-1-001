Our Treatment Techniques

Advanced physiotherapy modalities for effective pain relief and recovery

❄️

Cryotherapy / Cold Therapy

Controlled cold application to reduce inflammation, numb sharp pain, and speed up recovery. Ideal for acute injuries, swelling, and post-operative care. The cold therapy constricts blood vessels and slows nerve activity, providing quick pain relief.

🔥

Heat Therapy Treatment

Targeted heat application to relax tense muscles, improve blood circulation, and promote healing. Effective for chronic muscle pain, stiffness, and arthritis. Heat therapy increases oxygen flow and nutrient delivery to damaged tissues.

⚡

TENS Therapy

Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation uses low-voltage electrical currents to interfere with pain signals traveling to the brain. A non-invasive, drug-free approach to managing chronic and acute pain conditions effectively.

🦴

Spinal Disorder Treatment

Specialized programs for herniated discs, spondylosis, sciatica, and other spinal conditions. Our approach combines manual therapy, traction, and targeted exercises to restore spinal health and mobility.

🏃

Joint Pain Management

Comprehensive evaluate-and-treat programs for knee pain, shoulder issues, hip problems, and other joint conditions. We focus on restoring range of motion, strengthening supporting muscles, and preventing recurrence.

🧘

Rehabilitation Programs

Customized rehabilitation plans for post-surgical recovery, sports injuries, and chronic conditions. Our step-by-step programs guide you from pain management through to full functional recovery and prevention.
